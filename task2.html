How It Works

• Clicking the "Red" button sets the background to red using: document.body.style.backgroundColor = "red"

• Clicking the "Green" button sets the background to green using: document.body.style.backgroundColor = "green"

• Clicking the "Blue" button sets the background to blue using: document.body.style.backgroundColor = "blue"
